Brochure Design

On two separate occasions over a year, I had the pleasure of working with VW SA. The projects involved me designing brochure covers for logistics plan documents that related to national and international product launches. Some of these would then be customised with individual names and used as part of the media launch events.

The projects provided me with a healthy challenge while working with a discerning client. They required me to work with provided product photography as a starting point then producing the necessary concept drafts, before narrowing in on the final design.
